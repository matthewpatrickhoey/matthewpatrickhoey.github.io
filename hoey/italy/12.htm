Margaret STRAZZA

  • Born: 13 Dec 1891, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 1
  • Marriage (1): Filippo IALENTI Sr. in 1910 in St. Mary Of The Mount, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 1
  • Died: 7 May 1954, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 1 2 3
  • Buried: 10 May 1954, Mt. Carmel Cemetery, Verona, Allegheny, PA 2 4

Margaret was born on Mount Washington in either 18902 or 1891.1

She attended Prospect School through the eighth grade.1

She is listed in both the 1900 4 and 1910 census living with her parents,5 although she married sometime in that year.  She is later listed with her husband in the 1920 6 and 1930 census 7

By 1940, she was widowed and living in Homewood with ten children.8  She appeared in the 1950 census on 13 Apr 1950 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 10


Margaret married Filippo IALENTI Sr., son of Pasquale IALENTI and Michela MATTAROCCHIA, in 1910 in St. Mary Of The Mount, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA.1 (Filippo IALENTI Sr. was born on 23 Aug 1885 in Campolieto, Campobasso, Molise, Italy,11 12 13 died on 27 Jan 1937 in Penn Township, Allegheny, PA 1 3 11 and was buried on 30 Jan 1937 in Mt. Carmel Cemetery, Verona, Allegheny, PA 4.)
