Michael J. WALSH

  • Born: 29 Sep 1887, Galway, Ireland 2
  • Marriage: Rose Ellen O'DONNELL Abt 1919 1
  • Died: 1 Dec 1960, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 3

 He immigrated on 22 May 1902 to Boston, MA. 1 4 Michael arrived aboard the SS Ivernia which sailed from Queenstown on 14 May 1902 along with his mother and siblings.

 He served in the army during Workl War I. 5 Michael was in the Headquarters Company of the 320th Infantry Division and was gassed during one battle while saving the life of another soldier.

 He appeared in the 1920 census on 14 Jan 1920 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 6

 He appeared in the 1930 census on 17 Apr 1930 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 1

 He appeared in the 1940 census on 26 Apr 1940 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 7


Michael married Rose Ellen O'DONNELL, daughter of Unknown O'DONNELL and Mary MARSHALL, about 1919.1 (Rose Ellen O'DONNELL was born on 11 Jan 1892 in Pennsylvania 8 9 and died on 20 Nov 1974 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 10.)
