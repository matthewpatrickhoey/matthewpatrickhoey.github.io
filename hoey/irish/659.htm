Anne HOEY

  • Born: 29 Apr 1835, Kilcummin, Mayo, Ireland 2
  • Marriage (1): John NOLAN 1
  • Died: 26 Mar 1915, Port Chester, Westchester, NY 2

Anne emigrated about 1847 from Ireland. 2 She made the journey to America several years after her mother died and her father remarried. To read more about her life and voyage go to Absolutely Amy Anne, a genealogy blog done by Anne's 3rd Great Granddaughter.


Anne married John NOLAN.1 (John NOLAN was born on 11 Jan 1821 in Roscommon, Ireland 2 3 and died on 14 Oct 1880 in Rye, Westchester, NY 2 3.)
