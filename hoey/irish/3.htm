James Neal Nicholas HOEY

  • Born: 25 May 1933, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 1
  • Died: 13 Jun 2014, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 2
  • Buried: 18 Jun 2014, Calvary Cemetery, Pittsburgh, PA 2

 He appeared in the 1940 census on 15 Apr 1940 in Bethel Township, Allegheny, PA. 3

 James attended elementary school at St. Agnes in 1947, in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 1

 James attended high school at Central Catholic from Sep 1948 to May 1952, in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 1

 James attended college at Carnegie Technical Institute from Sep 1952 to May 1956, in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 1 Jim majored in civil engineering

 He served in the Army from 1957 to 1958. 1 Jim was in the Army Corps of Engineers and was stationed at the Presidio in San Francisco, CA.


James married Rosemary Lee WEET, daughter of Herbert Peter WEET Sr. and Julianna Rose STARK.
