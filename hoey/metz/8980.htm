Genovesa married Simon MEDER, son of Mathias MEDER and Agnes FRANCZ, on 11 Feb 1838 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Simon MEDER was born on 26 Jul 1808 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3.)


Genovesa next married Josephus EIBEN, son of Josephus EIBEN and Rosalia STARK, on 14 May 1848 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Josephus EIBEN was born about 1817 1 and died on 14 Dec 1862 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
