Vendelin KUNDT

  • Born: 20 Oct 1802, Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (1): Anna KRUPITZER on 25 Jul 1825 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Agnes EIBEN on 16 Aug 1835 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Vendelin married Anna KRUPITZER, daughter of Franciscus KRUPITZER and Juditha GOBL, on 25 Jul 1825 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna KRUPITZER was born on 5 Nov 1807 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 3 and died on 27 May 1835 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Vendelin next married Agnes EIBEN, daughter of Joannes EIBEN and Anna GEDEON, on 16 Aug 1835 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Agnes EIBEN was born on 9 Dec 1816 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 4 and died on 6 Feb 1873 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)
