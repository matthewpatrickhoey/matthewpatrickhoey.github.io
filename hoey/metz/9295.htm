Andreas SCHMOCZER

  • Born: 9 Feb 1813, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (1): Elisabetha ANTL on 1 May 1836 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Maria SCHÜRGER on 16 Jan 1853 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Andreas married Elisabetha ANTL, daughter of Martinus ANTL and Margaretha SCHMOCZER, on 1 May 1836 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Elisabetha ANTL was born on 7 Nov 1813 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2 and died on 7 May 1852 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Andreas next married Maria SCHÜRGER, daughter of Philippus SCHÜRGER and Theresia SCHMOCZER, on 16 Jan 1853 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Maria SCHÜRGER was born on 2 Sep 1828 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2.)
