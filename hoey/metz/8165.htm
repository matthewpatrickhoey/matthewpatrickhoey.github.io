Michael SCHUSTER

  • Born: Abt 1800 1
  • Marriage (1): Anna SCHÜRGER on 18 Jan 1829 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Agnes GRENCZER on 29 Jan 1837 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Died: 28 Feb 1855, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Michael married Anna SCHÜRGER, daughter of Vendelin SCHÜRGER and Anna ROOB, on 18 Jan 1829 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna SCHÜRGER was born on 5 Mar 1809 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3.)


Michael next married Agnes GRENCZER, daughter of Bartholome GRENCZER and Maria FRANCZ, on 29 Jan 1837 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Agnes GRENCZER was born on 25 Oct 1813 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 24 Nov 1869 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)
