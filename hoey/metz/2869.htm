Catharina married Andreas QUALLICH, son of Andreas QUALLICH and Anna Maria SCHOBERT, on 27 Oct 1835 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Andreas QUALLICH was born on 13 Aug 1814 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5 and died on 13 Feb 1850 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 6.)


Catharina next married Joannes KUNDT, son of Andreas KUNDT and Margaretha SCHÜRGER, on 8 Mar 1852 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Joannes KUNDT was born on 24 Jun 1815 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 5.)
