Michael GRENCZER

  • Born: Abt 1820 1 2
  • Marriage (1): Genovesa FRINDT on 29 Jan 1843 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Anna Maria MEDER on 4 Feb 1855 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Michael married Genovesa FRINDT, daughter of Joannes FRINDT and Anna FRIEDL, on 29 Jan 1843 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Genovesa FRINDT was born about 1824 1 and died on 12 Mar 1854 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Michael next married Anna Maria MEDER, daughter of Joannes EIBEN and Theresia MEDER, on 4 Feb 1855 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Anna Maria MEDER was born about 1829 2 and died on 21 Feb 1886 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
