Frances Suzanna FOLDVARY

  • Born: 26 Feb 1889, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2 3
  • Baptized: 2 Mar 1889, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 3
  • Marriage (1): George C. KRÄMER on 18 Oct 1916 in St. Michael, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 1 2
  • Died: 26 Dec 1953, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 1 2

• Immigration, 3 Oct 1904, Ellis Island, NY. 4 5 Frances was brought to the US by her uncle, Anton Stark, along
with her brother John. They arrived at Ellis Island, NY aboard the Franz Joseph.

When she got to Pittsburgh she worked in Anton's meat market on the South Side.


Alternate story from second source:
Frances Foldvary was brought to the U.S. by Dorothy Quallich. Dorothy's son John, married Agnes Stark.

• Census: 1930, 14 Apr 1930, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 6
