Mathias next married Sophia ORIENT, daughter of Josephus ORIENT and Anna Maria GLOSNER, on 20 Jan 1861 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Sophia ORIENT was born on 14 Apr 1836 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
