Benedictus FRIEDL

  • Born: 21 Mar 1830, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Maria SCHMOCZER on 6 Oct 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Agnes STARK on 1 Feb 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Died: 20 Nov 1895 3

Benedictus married Maria SCHMOCZER, daughter of Michael SCHMOCZER and Catharina GRENCZER, on 6 Oct 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Maria SCHMOCZER was born about 1830 1 and died on 26 Aug 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Benedictus next married Agnes STARK, daughter of Stephanus STARK and Agnes STARK, on 1 Feb 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Agnes STARK was born on 11 Jan 1845 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 26 Nov 1906 3.)
