Joannes FRANCZ

Joannes married Rosina FRINDT, daughter of Joannes FRINDT and Anna FRIEDL, on 16 Jan 1848 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Rosina FRINDT was born about 1828 1 and died on 17 Nov 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Joannes next married Magdalena EIBEN, daughter of Jacob EIBEN and Anna SCHMOCZER, on 20 Jan 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Magdalena EIBEN was born on 17 Jul 1832 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 4 and died on 19 Jan 1879 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)


Joannes next married Theresia MEDER, daughter of Michael MEDER and Agnes GRENCZER, on 8 Sep 1885 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Theresia MEDER was born on 7 Oct 1834 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 4 and died on 18 Jun 1897 4.)
