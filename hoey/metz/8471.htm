Catharina SCHMOCZER

  • Born: 17 Sep 1844, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Philippus FRANCZ on 16 Feb 1868 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Joannes VINDT on 9 Jul 1876 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Died: 26 Jul 1901 3

Catharina married Philippus FRANCZ, son of Thomas FRANCZ and Maria ANTL, on 16 Feb 1868 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Philippus FRANCZ was born on 30 Apr 1837 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 7 Jan 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Catharina next married Joannes VINDT, son of Andreas VINDT and Margaretha FRINDT, on 9 Jul 1876 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Joannes VINDT was born on 8 Aug 1842 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
