Anna Maria GRENCZER

  • Born: Abt 1850 1
  • Marriage (1): Aloysius KRUPITZER on 12 Nov 1878 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Josephus SORGER on 21 Nov 1880 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (3): Joannes BUKOVSZKY on 16 Jan 1881 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Anna married Aloysius KRUPITZER, son of Michael KRUPITZER and Anna Maria SCHÜRGER, on 12 Nov 1878 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Aloysius KRUPITZER was born on 21 Jun 1841 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 12 Nov 1878 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Anna next married Josephus SORGER on 21 Nov 1880 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Josephus SORGER was born about 1853 1.)


Anna next married Joannes BUKOVSZKY, son of Joannes BUKOVSZKY and Mechtildus SCHMOCZER, on 16 Jan 1881 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Joannes BUKOVSZKY was born on 16 Feb 1854 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)
