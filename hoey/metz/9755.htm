Dorothea EIBEN

  • Born: 23 May 1798, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2
  • Marriage (1): Andreas ROOB on 1 Sep 1822 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Joannes ROOB on 8 Feb 1829 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Dorothea married Andreas ROOB, son of Jacobus ROOB and Gertrudis MEDER, on 1 Sep 1822 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Andreas ROOB was born on 17 Nov 1757 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Dorothea next married Joannes ROOB, son of Casparus ROOB and Anna MEDER, on 8 Feb 1829 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Joannes ROOB was born on 19 Jun 1777 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 3 and died on 26 Aug 1831 in Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
