Magdalena FRIEDL

  • Born: 17 Jul 1829, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (1): Georgius MEDER on 30 Sep 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Philippus EIBEN on 23 Feb 1862 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Died: 1 May 1868, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3

Magdalena married Georgius MEDER, son of Joannes MEDER and Sophia GEDEON, on 30 Sep 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Georgius MEDER was born about 1824 1.)


Magdalena next married Philippus EIBEN, son of Mathias EIBEN and Agnes SCHMOCZER, on 23 Feb 1862 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Philippus EIBEN was born on 1 May 1830 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2 and died on 19 Sep 1892 4.)
