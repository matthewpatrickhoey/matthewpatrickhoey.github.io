Andreas GRENCZER

  • Born: 13 Nov 1844, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Anna Maria GRENCZER on 14 Aug 1870 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Victoria EIBEN on 4 May 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Andreas married Anna Maria GRENCZER, daughter of Franciscus GRENCZER and Magdalena MEDER, on 14 Aug 1870 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria GRENCZER was born on 7 Nov 1851 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 19 Jan 1872 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Andreas next married Victoria EIBEN, daughter of Josephus EIBEN and Genovesa GEDEON, on 4 May 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Victoria EIBEN was born on 23 Dec 1852 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
