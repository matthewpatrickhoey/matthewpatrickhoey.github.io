Andreas GRENCZER

  • Born: 26 Nov 1814, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2
  • Marriage (1): Anna Maria VAGNER on 17 Jan 1841 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Anna Maria REITZ on 15 Feb 1857 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Died: 16 Nov 1860, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3

Andreas married Anna Maria VAGNER, daughter of Martinus VAGNER and Anna Maria FRANCZ, on 17 Jan 1841 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria VAGNER was born on 1 Apr 1818 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 4 and died on 27 Jan 1857 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Andreas next married Anna Maria REITZ, daughter of Andreas REITZ and Apollonia SCHUSTER, on 15 Feb 1857 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria REITZ was born on 29 Mar 1832 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2.)
