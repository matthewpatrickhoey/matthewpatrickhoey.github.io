Victoria GEDEON

  • Born: 23 Dec 1848, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Joannes KUND on 16 Jan 1870 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Jacobus TOMASCH on 29 Apr 1890 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Victoria married Joannes KUND, son of Joannes Florianus KUND and Anna Maria GEDEON, on 16 Jan 1870 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Joannes KUND was born on 24 Oct 1842 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 27 Mar 1876 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Victoria next married Jacobus TOMASCH on 29 Apr 1890 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Jacobus TOMASCH was born about 1837 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)
