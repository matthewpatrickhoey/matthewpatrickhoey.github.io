Joannes MEDER

  • Born: 23 Jun 1848, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Agnes VOLF on 23 Feb 1868 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Joannes married Agnes VOLF, daughter of Joannes VOLF and Maria SCHMOCZER, on 23 Feb 1868 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Agnes VOLF was born on 21 Apr 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 14 Nov 1886 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Joannes next married Susanna SCHMOCZER, daughter of Casparus SCHMOCZER and Agnes FRIEDL, on 14 May 1888 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Susanna SCHMOCZER was born on 11 Aug 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
