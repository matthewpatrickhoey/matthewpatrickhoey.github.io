Anna Maria ORIENT

  • Born: 14 Nov 1810, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (1): Michael EIBEN on 13 Jul 1828 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Simon ANTL on 21 Jan 1844 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Anna married Michael EIBEN, son of Michael EIBEN and Catharina SCHMOCZER, on 13 Jul 1828 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Michael EIBEN was born on 23 Sep 1802 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3.)


Anna next married Simon ANTL, son of Anton ANTL and Theresia KLEIN, on 21 Jan 1844 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Simon ANTL was born on 28 Oct 1802 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3 and died on 13 Feb 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
