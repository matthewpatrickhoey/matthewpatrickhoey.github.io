Louis GEDEON

  • Born: 19 Sep 1877, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 1
  • Died: 11 Oct 1950, Washington, DC 1
  • Buried: National Cemetery, Washington, DC 1

• Census: 1880, 18 Jun 1880, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 2

• Military Service: Army. 3 Louis served as a private in Company G, 19th U.S. Infantry and saw action in the Philippines.

• Military Award: Congressional Medal of Honor, 10 Mar 1902. 3 At Mount Amia, Cebu, Philippine Islands, 4 February 1900 he "singlehandedy defended his mortally wounded captain from an overwhelming force of the enemy."
