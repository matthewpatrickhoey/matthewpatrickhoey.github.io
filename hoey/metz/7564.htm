Joannes married Theresia SCHMOCZER on 12 Jan 1862 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Theresia SCHMOCZER was born about 1841 1 and died on 31 Jul 1864 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Joannes next married Sophia ORIENT, daughter of Jacobus ORIENT and Sophia FOEDER, on 15 Jan 1865 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Sophia ORIENT was born on 28 Apr 1841 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 13 Dec 1866 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Joannes next married Anna Maria MEDER, daughter of Joannes MEDER and Anna Maria SZTRAMMER, on 17 Feb 1867 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria MEDER was born on 29 Nov 1845 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 30 Sep 1912 2.)
