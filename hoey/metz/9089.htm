Magdalena MEDER

  • Born: Abt 1829 1
  • Marriage (1): Franciscus GRENCZER on 21 Nov 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Philippus GEDEON on 12 Nov 1854 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Magdalena married Franciscus GRENCZER, son of Joannes GRENCZER and Catharina ANTL, on 21 Nov 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Franciscus GRENCZER was born about 1824 1 and died on 6 Jun 1853 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Magdalena next married Philippus GEDEON on 12 Nov 1854 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Philippus GEDEON was born about 1829 1 and died on 31 Jul 1882 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
