Conrad EIBEN

  • Born: 4 May 1880, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2
  • Died: 6 Apr 1954, Springfield, MA 2 3 4
  • Buried: St. Paul's Monastary Cemetery, Pittsburgh, PA 3

 He emigrated about 1887 from Austria-Hungary. 2

 He was ordained on 30 Jun 1906. 2  While a priest at St. Michael's in Pittsburgh, Conrad helped to create Veronica's Veil.  He was also later the pastor at St. Michael's.

 He appeared in the 1900 census on 27 Jan 1900 in Highland, Jefferson, Kentucky. 5

 He appeared in the 1910 census on 15 Apr 1910 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 6

 He appeared in the 1920 census on 18 Jan 1920 in West Hoboken, Hudson, NJ. 7

 He appeared in the 1930 census on 16 Apr 1930 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 8
