Barbara married Casparus GEDEON on 29 Oct 1854 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Casparus GEDEON was born about 1830 1 and died on 30 Aug 1878 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Barbara next married Joannes GRENCZER, son of Valentinus GRENCZER and Sophia FRIEDL, on 4 Jun 1888 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Joannes GRENCZER was born on 23 Jan 1837 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 21 Feb 1901 3.)
