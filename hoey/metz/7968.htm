Theresia married Michael FOEDER, son of Michael FOEDER and Anna ANTL, on 8 Oct 1823 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Michael FOEDER was born on 27 Sep 1799 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2.)


Theresia next married Georgius FRINDT, son of Joannes FRINDT and Maria GANDALOVITS, on 6 Nov 1831 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Georgius FRINDT was born on 9 May 1802 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2.)


Theresia next married Josephus SCHMOCZER, son of Josephus SCHMOCZER and Catharina TAUB, on 7 Jan 1833 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Josephus SCHMOCZER was born on 6 Apr 1796 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2.)


Theresia next married Philippus EIBEN, son of Casparus EIBEN and Anna Maria GAMPE, on 16 Apr 1837 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Philippus EIBEN was born on 30 Apr 1807 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 4 5 and died on 29 Nov 1868 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
