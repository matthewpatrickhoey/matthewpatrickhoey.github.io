Philippus married Theresia GEDEON, daughter of Joannes GEDEON and Theresia MEDER, on 14 Jul 1831 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Theresia GEDEON was born on 19 Sep 1813 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Philippus next married Theresia SCHMOCZER, daughter of Andreas SCHMOCZER and Maria SCHMOCZER, on 16 Apr 1837 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Theresia SCHMOCZER was born on 24 Jan 1806 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 5 and died on 30 Mar 1863 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
