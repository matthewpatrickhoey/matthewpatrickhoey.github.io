Michael next married Theresia MEDER, daughter of Michael MEDER and Agnes GRENCZER, on 4 Feb 1872 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Theresia MEDER was born on 7 Oct 1834 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2 and died on 18 Jun 1897 2.)
