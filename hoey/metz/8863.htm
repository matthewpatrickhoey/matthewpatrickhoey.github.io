Sabina SCHMOCZER

  • Born: Abt 1829 1
  • Marriage (1): Vendelin DEUTCHER on 21 Nov 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Josephus EIBEN on 26 Jan 1851 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Sabina married Vendelin DEUTCHER on 21 Nov 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Vendelin DEUTCHER was born about 1824 1 and died on 20 Aug 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Sabina next married Josephus EIBEN, son of Joannes EIBEN and Barbara GEDEON, on 26 Jan 1851 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Josephus EIBEN was born about 1815 1 and died on 10 Jan 1852 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Sabina next married Thomas FRANCZ, son of Michael FRANCZ and Elisabetha KATRINGER, on 13 May 1856 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Thomas FRANCZ was born on 14 Dec 1813 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 7 Mar 1862 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Sabina next married Michael KLEIN, son of Candidus KLEIN and Barbara MEDER, on 26 Feb 1865 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Michael KLEIN was born about 1825 1.)
