Joannes next married Theresia SCHOLCZ, daughter of Anton SCHOLCZ and Theresia SCHMOCZER, on 20 Jan 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Theresia SCHOLCZ was born about 1813 1 and died on 15 Aug 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
