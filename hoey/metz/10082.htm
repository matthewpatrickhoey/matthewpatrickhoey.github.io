Sabina married Michael REITZ, son of Philippus REITZ and Anna GRENCZER, on 22 Jul 1827 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Michael REITZ was born on 26 Sep 1806 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 4 and died on 7 May 1842 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Sabina next married Philippus GRENCZER, son of Bartholome GRENCZER and Theresia SCHMOCZER, on 23 Sep 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Philippus GRENCZER was born on 30 Apr 1810 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 8 Feb 1863 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
