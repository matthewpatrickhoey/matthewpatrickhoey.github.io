Anna Maria DUKK

  • Born: 18 Nov 1860, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (1): Casparus MEDER on 17 Jan 1886 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Casparus EIBEN on 8 Jun 1891 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Died: 25 May 1903 2

Anna married Casparus MEDER, son of Joannes MEDER and Apollonia GEDEON, on 17 Jan 1886 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Casparus MEDER was born on 7 Jan 1858 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 12 Apr 1890 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Anna next married Casparus EIBEN, son of Joannes EIBEN and Anna Maria GINDL, on 8 Jun 1891 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Casparus EIBEN was born on 23 Dec 1866 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)
