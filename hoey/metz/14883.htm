Franciscus married Sophia SCHÜRGER, daughter of Casparus SCHÜRGER and Julianna SCHOBERT, on 11 Jul 1841 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Sophia SCHÜRGER was born on 1 Apr 1819 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 29 Apr 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Franciscus next married Barbara TENDRICH, daughter of Joannes TENDRICH and Sophia BÖHM, on 30 May 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Barbara TENDRICH was born on 30 Nov 1823 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 26 Nov 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Franciscus next married Magdalena FRANCZ, daughter of Salomon FRANCZ and Catharina EIBEN, on 13 Jan 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Magdalena FRANCZ was born on 10 Jul 1822 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 18 Jun 1853 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Franciscus next married Anna Maria EIBEN, daughter of Laurentius EIBEN and Maria EIBEN, on 22 Jan 1854 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria EIBEN was born on 14 Apr 1833 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 1 Jan 1894 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
