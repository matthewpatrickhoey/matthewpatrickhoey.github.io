Theresia SCHMOCZER

  • Born: 14 Sep 1843, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Michael STARK on 19 Jan 1862 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Georgius MEDER on 3 May 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Died: 16 Oct 1874, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4

Theresia married Michael STARK, son of Jacobus STARK and Theresia FRIEDL, on 19 Jan 1862 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Michael STARK was born on 23 Sep 1837 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 11 Dec 1872 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)


Theresia next married Georgius MEDER, son of Albertus MEDER and Theresia FRIEDL, on 3 May 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Georgius MEDER was born on 8 Apr 1851 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 27 Jul 1885 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
