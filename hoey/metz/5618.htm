Anna Maria STARK

  • Born: 30 Jun 1842, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Mathias FRANCZ on 12 May 1867 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Josephus MEDER on 26 May 1878 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (3): Joannes ZAVADOSZKY on 22 May 1882 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (4): Mathias POTRA on 17 Aug 1885 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Anna married Mathias FRANCZ, son of Georgius FRANCZ and Anna EIBEN, on 12 May 1867 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Mathias FRANCZ was born about 1828 1 and died on 5 Aug 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Anna next married Josephus MEDER, son of Michael MEDER and Maria FOEDER, on 26 May 1878 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Josephus MEDER was born about 1827 2 and died on 13 Apr 1882 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Anna next married Joannes ZAVADOSZKY, son of Mathias ZAVADOSZKY and Anna Maria MEDER, on 22 May 1882 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Joannes ZAVADOSZKY was born on 14 Jun 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 18 Mar 1883 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Anna next married Mathias POTRA, son of Joannes POTRA and Anna Maria FRANCZ, on 17 Aug 1885 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Mathias POTRA was born on 16 Feb 1826 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5 and died on 25 Apr 1894 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)
