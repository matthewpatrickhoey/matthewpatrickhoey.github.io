Matthew Joseph WEET

  • Born: 17 May 1900, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 2
  • Marriage: (1): Rosemary BUCKLEY Aft 1923 1
  • Marriage: (2): Janette MANN Aft 1941 1
  • Died: Aft 11 Jan 1945 3 4

 He has conflicting birth information of May 1899 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 1 5

 He appeared in the 1900 census on 7 Jun 1900 in Wheeling, Ohio, West Virginia. 5

 He appeared in the 1910 census on 30 Apr 1910 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 6

 He appeared in the 1920 census on 5 Jan 1920 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 7

 He appeared in the 1930 census on 3 Apr 1930 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 8

 He served in the Army. 4 Matthew served during World War II, although he was over the age of service. He did not go overseas.

 On January 11, 1945 Matthew walked to the store to get some things that his wife wanted. He never came home. Janette called relatives who searched the area. The police were called and they also were unable to find any trace of him. Matthew had been in a car accident several weeks prior to his disappearance and the family speculate that he developed amnesia.4


Matthew married Rosemary BUCKLEY after 1923.1 (Rosemary BUCKLEY died on 5 May 1941 1.)

Matthew also married Janette MANN, daughter of William MANN and Rose A. (MANN), after 1941.1 (Janette MANN was born on 18 Oct 1904 in Pennsylvania 9 10 and died on 15 Oct 2002 in Clearfield, PA 3.)
