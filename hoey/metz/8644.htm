Anna married Andreas GEDEON, son of Joannes GEDEON and Dorothea KOSCH, on 10 Nov 1844 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Andreas GEDEON was born about 1822 1 and died on 26 Jan 1856 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Anna next married Thomas NEUMANN, son of Thomas NEUMANN and Sophia EIBEN, on 6 Sep 1857 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Thomas NEUMANN was born on 5 Mar 1832 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3 and died on 10 Sep 1870 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)
