Thomas FRANCZ

  • Born: 14 Dec 1813, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (1): Maria ANTL on 18 Jan 1835 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Margaretha VAGNER on 20 Jan 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (3): Sabina SCHMOCZER on 13 May 1856 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Died: 7 Mar 1862, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3

Thomas married Maria ANTL, daughter of Canutus ANTL and Maria SCHMOCZER, on 18 Jan 1835 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Maria ANTL was born on 12 Sep 1816 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2 and died on 18 Nov 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Thomas next married Margaretha VAGNER, daughter of Martinus VAGNER and Anna Maria FRANCZ, on 20 Jan 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Margaretha VAGNER was born on 11 May 1811 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 4 and died on 3 Feb 1856 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Thomas next married Sabina SCHMOCZER, daughter of Salomon SCHMOCZER and Elisabetha FRINDT, on 13 May 1856 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Sabina SCHMOCZER was born about 1829 1.)
