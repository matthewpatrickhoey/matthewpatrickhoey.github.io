Paulina STARK

  • Born: 28 May 1866, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Baptized: 29 May 1866, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Ludovicus VITKOVSZKY on 25 Apr 1886 in St. Michael, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 1 2
  • Died: 27 Apr 1931, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 4 5
  • Buried: 30 Apr 1931, St. George Cemetery, Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 4 5 6

Paulina's marriage license application as well as her grave marker lists her birth date as 29 Nov 1867. 3 5 This date can be disproven by her birth record entry in the Ober-Metzenseifen church records.

She emigrated in 1882 from Austria-Hungary. 6 7 Several brothers and sisters came over with her, although it is not known how many stayed in the States.

She appeared in the 1900 census on 7 Jun 1900 in Wheeling, Ohio, West Virginia. 6

She appeared in the 1910 census on 30 Apr 1910 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 8

She appeared in the 1920 census on 5 Jan 1920 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 9

She appeared in the 1930 census on 3 Apr 1930 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 10
