Franciscus SCHMOCZER

  • Born: 2 Dec 1844, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Elisabetha FRINDT on 18 Nov 1866 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Maria SCHMOCZER on 23 Nov 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Died: 14 Feb 1901, Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4

Franciscus married Elisabetha FRINDT, daughter of Joannes FRINDT and Anna EIBEN, on 18 Nov 1866 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Elisabetha FRINDT was born on 13 Nov 1848 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 16 Jul 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)


Franciscus next married Maria SCHMOCZER, daughter of Georgius SCHMOCZER and Anna Maria GRENCZER, on 23 Nov 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Maria SCHMOCZER was born on 3 Aug 1856 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 4.)
