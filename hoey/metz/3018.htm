Jacob married Anna SCHMOCZER on 22 Jan 1815 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 2


Jacob next married Elisabetha SCHMOCZER, daughter of Godefridus SCHMOCZER and Theresia SCHMOCZER, on 12 May 1839 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Elisabetha SCHMOCZER was born on 30 Nov 1794 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 3.)


Jacob next married Elisabetha MEDER, daughter of Joannes MEDER and Catharina GRENCZER, on 20 Aug 1843 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Elisabetha MEDER was born on 16 Nov 1806 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 3 and died on 1 Dec 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
