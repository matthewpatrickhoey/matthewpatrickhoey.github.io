Magdalena FOEDER

  • Born: Abt 1840 1

Magdalena married Franciscus FRIEDL, son of Michael FRIEDL and Anna Maria SCHMOCZER. (Franciscus FRIEDL was born on 29 Mar 1828 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 1 Apr 1871 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Magdalena next married Franciscus SCHMOCZER, son of Jacobus SCHMOCZER and Theresia FRIEDL, on 11 Jun 1872 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Franciscus SCHMOCZER was born on 29 Dec 1848 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)
