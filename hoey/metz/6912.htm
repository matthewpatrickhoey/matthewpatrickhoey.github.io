Joannes married Agnes QUALLICH, daughter of Simon QUALLICH and Agnes TISCHLER, on 12 Jan 1840 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Agnes QUALLICH was born on 8 Jan 1824 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Joannes next married Catharina SORGER on 8 Mar 1852 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Catharina SORGER was born about 1817 4 and died on 3 Jan 1868 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
