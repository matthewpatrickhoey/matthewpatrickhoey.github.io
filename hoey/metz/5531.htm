Philippus EIBEN

  • Born: 22 May 1835, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Sophia SCHMOCZER on 15 Jan 1860 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Lucretia EIBEN on 26 Oct 1880 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Died: 3 Jun 1881, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3

Philippus married Sophia SCHMOCZER, daughter of Joannes SCHMOCZER and Elisabetha GEDEON, on 15 Jan 1860 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Sophia SCHMOCZER was born about 1841 1 and died on 1 Dec 1879 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Philippus next married Lucretia EIBEN, daughter of Joannes EIBEN and Catharina EIBEN, on 26 Oct 1880 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Lucretia EIBEN was born on 3 May 1842 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 5 May 1903 3.)
