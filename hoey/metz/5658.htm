Agnes STARK

  • Born: 11 Jan 1845, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Joannes MEDER on 20 Jan 1867 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Benedictus FRIEDL on 1 Feb 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Died: 26 Nov 1906 3

Agnes married Joannes MEDER, son of Joannes MEDER and Elisabetha KNERSCIK, on 20 Jan 1867 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Joannes MEDER was born on 29 Aug 1839 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Agnes next married Benedictus FRIEDL, son of Casparus FRIEDL and Rosalia ROOB, on 1 Feb 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Benedictus FRIEDL was born on 21 Mar 1830 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 20 Nov 1895 3.)
