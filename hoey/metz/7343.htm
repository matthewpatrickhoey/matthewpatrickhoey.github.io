Anna Maria GRENCZER

  • Born: Abt 1810 1
  • Marriage (1): Casparus FRIEDL on 1 Mar 1829 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Joannes FRINDT on 29 Apr 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Died: 5 Feb 1869, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Anna married Casparus FRIEDL, son of Casparus FRIEDL and Sophia MEDER, on 1 Mar 1829 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Casparus FRIEDL was born on 12 Oct 1805 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 5 Feb 1843 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Anna next married Joannes FRINDT, son of Joannes FRINDT and Maria GANDALOVITS, on 29 Apr 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Joannes FRINDT was born on 15 Nov 1805 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3 and died on 11 Aug 1869 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)
