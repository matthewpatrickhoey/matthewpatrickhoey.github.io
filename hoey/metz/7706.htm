Joannes married Anna Maria GEDEON, daughter of Joannes GEDEON and Elisabetha FRINDT, on 17 Jan 1864 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria GEDEON was born on 7 Dec 1840 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 12 Oct 1865 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)


Joannes next married Anna Maria SCHUSTER, daughter of Michael SCHUSTER and Agnes GRENCZER, on 29 Apr 1866 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria SCHUSTER was born on 9 Nov 1840 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 21 Dec 1872 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)


Joannes next married Rosalia STARK, daughter of Thomas STARK and Anna Maria REITZ, on 4 May 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Rosalia STARK was born on 22 Aug 1836 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 30 Apr 1908 3.)
