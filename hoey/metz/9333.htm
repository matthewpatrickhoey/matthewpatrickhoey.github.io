Charitas GEDEON

  • Born: Abt 1818 1
  • Marriage (1): Augustinius SCHMOCZER on 25 Nov 1838 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Jacobus SCHOLCZ on 20 Jan 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Charitas married Augustinius SCHMOCZER, son of Georgius SCHMOCZER and Anna SCHÜRGER, on 25 Nov 1838 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Augustinius SCHMOCZER was born about 1814 1 and died on 5 Feb 1844 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Charitas next married Jacobus SCHOLCZ, son of Michael SCHOLCZ and Elisabetha BERES, on 20 Jan 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Jacobus SCHOLCZ was born on 26 Apr 1807 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 4 and died on 9 Jan 1855 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)
