Casparus next married Elisabetha REITZ, daughter of Joannes REITZ and Elisabetha STODOLNIK, on 2 Oct 1831 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Elisabetha REITZ was born on 26 Oct 1798 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3 and died on 2 Jan 1872 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)
