• Catherine went to Germany in 1962 along with Mildred (Goldbach) Albrecht.  They went to visit Mildred's son Larry and his wife Ruth (Weet).  While in Germany, she visited relatives from Metzenseifen, including Gazella Roob.  While there, Gazella's daughter, Sylvia, was married12.  It was during this trip that she began to work on the original Weet Family Tree.


Catherine married John James ORIENT, son of Joseph Francis ORIENT and Catherina SCHMOCZER. (John James ORIENT was born on 31 Dec 1888 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA,11 died on 10 Nov 1936 in Carnegie, Allegheny, PA 3 and was buried in St. Joseph Cemetery, Carnegie, Allegheny, PA 5.)
