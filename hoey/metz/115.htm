Catherine Sarah WEET

 She appeared in the 1900 census on 7 Jun 1900 in Wheeling, Ohio, West Virginia. 6

 She appeared in the 1910 census on 30 Apr 1910 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA. 7

 She appeared in the 1920 census on 13 Feb 1920 in Upper St. Clair Township, Allegheny, PA. 8

 She appeared in the 1930 census on 25 Apr 1930 in Upper St. Clair Township, Allegheny, PA. 9

 Catherine went to Germany in 1962 along with Mildred (Goldbach) Albrecht.  They went to visit Mildred's son Larry and his wife Ruth (Weet).  While in Germany, she visited relatives from Metzenseifen, including Gazella Roob.  While there, Gazella's daughter, Sylvia, was married11.  It was during this trip that she began to work on the original Weet Family Tree.


Catherine married John James ORIENT, son of Joseph Francis ORIENT and Catherina SCHMOTZER. (John James ORIENT was born on 31 Dec 1888 in Pittsburgh, Allegheny, PA 10, died on 10 Nov 1936 in Carnegie, Allegheny, PA 3 and was buried in St. Joseph Cemetery, Carnegie, PA 5.)
