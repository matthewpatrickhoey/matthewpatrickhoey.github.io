Anna married Vendelin EIBEN, son of Michael EIBEN and Agnes GRENCZER, on 9 Feb 1840 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Vendelin EIBEN was born about 1818 1 and died on 16 Jan 1856 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Anna next married Jacobus TOMASOVSZKY on 14 May 1861 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Jacobus TOMASOVSZKY was born about 1812 1 and died on 23 Mar 1888 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
