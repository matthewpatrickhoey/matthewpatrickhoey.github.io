Joannes VINDT

Joannes married Anna SCHMOCZER, daughter of Martinus SCHMOCZER and Johanna CZECHNER, on 18 Sep 1864 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna SCHMOCZER was born on 29 Apr 1847 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 18 Feb 1876 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Joannes next married Catharina SCHMOCZER, daughter of Joachimus SCHMOCZER and Anna Maria ANTL, on 9 Jul 1876 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Catharina SCHMOCZER was born on 17 Sep 1844 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 26 Jul 1901 3.)
