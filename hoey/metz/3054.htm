Catharina SCHMOCZER

  • Born: 26 Nov 1818, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3 4
  • Marriage (1): Georgius MONICH on 6 Nov 1836 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2
  • Marriage (2): Mathias POTRA on 26 Oct 1845 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Died: 24 Jun 1885, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 5

Catharina married Georgius MONICH, son of Joannes MONICH and Susanna SCHMOCZER, on 6 Nov 1836 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 2 (Georgius MONICH was born on 3 Apr 1817 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1.)


Catharina next married Mathias POTRA, son of Joannes POTRA and Anna Maria FRANCZ, on 26 Oct 1845 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.3 (Mathias POTRA was born on 16 Feb 1826 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 25 Apr 1894 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
