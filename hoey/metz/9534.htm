Andreas EIBEN

  • Marriage (2): Barbara MEDER on 8 Sep 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Andreas married Magdalena GEDEON, daughter of Martinus GEDEON and Juditha TISCHLER, on 10 Feb 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Magdalena GEDEON was born on 4 Jul 1833 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2 and died on 17 Jun 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Andreas next married Barbara MEDER, daughter of Joannes MEDER and Theresia SCHMOCZER, on 8 Sep 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Barbara MEDER was born on 3 Dec 1832 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2 and died on 5 Dec 1884 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
