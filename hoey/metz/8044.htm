Anna HUBLER

  • Born: 23 Jul 1853, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (1): Josephus KOLESAR on 21 Nov 1880 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Alexander DEMARCSIK on 21 Feb 1886 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Anna married Josephus KOLESAR, son of Georgius KOLESAR and Genovesa TOMASOVSZKY, on 21 Nov 1880 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Josephus KOLESAR was born on 1 Mar 1857 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Anna next married Alexander DEMARCSIK, son of Michael DEMARCSIK and Susanna FRIEDL, on 21 Feb 1886 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Alexander DEMARCSIK was born on 30 Jan 1857 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 12 Jan 1905 2.)
