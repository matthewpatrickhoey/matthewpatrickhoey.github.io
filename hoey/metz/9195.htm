Martinus SCHIMKOLA

  • Born: Abt 1808 1
  • Marriage (1): Elisabetha MEDER on 3 Feb 1833 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Agnes HORVATH on 11 Oct 1846 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Died: 5 Jan 1850, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Martinus married Elisabetha MEDER, daughter of Georgius MEDER and Anna SCHMOCZER, on 3 Feb 1833 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Elisabetha MEDER was born about 1811 1 and died on 29 Nov 1845 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Martinus next married Agnes HORVATH, daughter of Martinus HORVATH and Genovesa FRANCZ, on 11 Oct 1846 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Agnes HORVATH was born on 27 Sep 1815 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 22 Dec 1886 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
