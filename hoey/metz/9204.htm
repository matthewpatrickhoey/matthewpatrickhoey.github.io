Josephus SCHMOCZER

  • Born: 18 Mar 1839, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 4
  • Marriage (1): Agnes SCHUSTER on 29 Apr 1867 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Theresia MEDER on 4 May 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Died: 26 Jan 1890, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5

Josephus married Agnes SCHUSTER, daughter of Emericus SCHUSTER and Sophia IMLING, on 29 Apr 1867 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Agnes SCHUSTER was born on 23 Mar 1851 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 6 and died on 20 Feb 1871 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 7.)


Josephus next married Theresia MEDER, daughter of Georgius MEDER and Magdalena FRIEDL, on 4 May 1873 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Theresia MEDER was born on 22 Oct 1853 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
