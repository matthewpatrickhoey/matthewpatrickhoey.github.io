Joannes SCHMOCZER

  • Born: 4 Sep 1839, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3
  • Marriage (1): Agnes SCHMOCZER on 16 Feb 1868 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Anna Maria CSILCSER on 22 Apr 1877 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Died: 12 Oct 1886, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4

Joannes married Agnes SCHMOCZER, daughter of Casparus SCHMOCZER and Agnes FRIEDL, on 16 Feb 1868 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Agnes SCHMOCZER was born on 31 Jul 1850 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 7 Apr 1876 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Joannes next married Anna Maria CSILCSER, daughter of Joannes CSILCSER and Anna Maria GEDEON, on 22 Apr 1877 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Anna Maria CSILCSER was born on 11 Apr 1857 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
