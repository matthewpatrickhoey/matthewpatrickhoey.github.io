Joachimus married Margaretha SCHMOCZER, daughter of Georgius SCHMOCZER and Anna SCHÜRGER, on 19 Aug 1832 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Margaretha SCHMOCZER was born on 8 Jul 1800 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Joachimus next married Anna Maria ANTL, daughter of Georgius ANTL and Dorothea EIBEN, on 3 Feb 1839 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria ANTL was born about 1805 1 and died on 1 Jan 1880 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
