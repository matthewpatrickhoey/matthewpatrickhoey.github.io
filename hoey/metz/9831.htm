Franciscus FOEDER

  • Born: 3 Oct 1799, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2
  • Marriage (1): Anna Maria FRANCZ on 6 Aug 1826 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Anna Maria SCHMOCZER on 20 May 1827 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Franciscus married Anna Maria FRANCZ, daughter of Joannes FRANCZ and Barbara SCHMOCZER, on 6 Aug 1826 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria FRANCZ was born on 11 Aug 1805 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2.)


Franciscus next married Anna Maria SCHMOCZER, daughter of Georgius SCHMOCZER and Dorothea SZTRAK, on 20 May 1827 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria SCHMOCZER was born on 10 Oct 1808 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3.)
