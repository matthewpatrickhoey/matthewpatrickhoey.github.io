Georgius SCHÜRGER

  • Born: 27 Apr 1822, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 3
  • Marriage (1): Anna Maria SCHMOCZER on 19 Feb 1843 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Theresia FRIEDL on 27 Jun 1867 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Georgius married Anna Maria SCHMOCZER, daughter of Thomas SCHMOCZER and Sophia FRANCZ, on 19 Feb 1843 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Anna Maria SCHMOCZER was born on 26 Jan 1822 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 3 and died on 18 Oct 1866 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Georgius next married Theresia FRIEDL, daughter of Mathias FRIEDL and Anna ZSACHANOVSKY, on 27 Jun 1867 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Theresia FRIEDL was born on 3 Oct 1818 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 9 Aug 1892 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 5.)
