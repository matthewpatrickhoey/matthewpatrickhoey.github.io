Georgius SCHIMKOLA

  • Born: 27 Mar 1815, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2
  • Marriage (1): Margaretha MEDER on 6 Oct 1833 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Genovesa GEDEON on 26 Oct 1840 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Died: 25 Aug 1857, Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3

Georgius married Margaretha MEDER, daughter of Augustinus MEDER and Catharina SCHMOCZER, on 6 Oct 1833 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Margaretha MEDER was born on 26 Oct 1814 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2.)


Georgius next married Genovesa GEDEON, daughter of Joannes GEDEON and Anna EIBEN, on 26 Oct 1840 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Genovesa GEDEON was born on 31 Dec 1817 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 13 Nov 1874 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
