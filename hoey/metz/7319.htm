Theresia FRIEDL

  • Marriage (2): Albertus MEDER on 2 Feb 1845 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Theresia married Jacobus STARK, son of Andreas STARK and Agnes EIBEN, on 11 Sep 1825 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Jacobus STARK was born on 24 Jul 1801 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4 and died on 10 Aug 1843 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)


Theresia next married Albertus MEDER, son of Mathias MEDER and Agnes FRANCZ, on 2 Feb 1845 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Albertus MEDER was born on 9 Jun 1806 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 4 and died on 23 Mar 1855 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3.)
