Michael MEDER

  • Born: Abt 1840 1
  • Marriage (1): Theresia EIBEN on 12 Nov 1865 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Agnes MULLER on 9 May 1887 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2

Michael married Theresia EIBEN, daughter of Michael EIBEN and Thecla FOEDER, on 12 Nov 1865 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Theresia EIBEN was born on 10 Oct 1842 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 26 Jan 1887 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Michael next married Agnes MULLER on 9 May 1887 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.2 (Agnes MULLER was born about 1853 2.)
