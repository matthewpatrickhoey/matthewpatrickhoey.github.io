Michael next married Susanna FRIEDL, daughter of Simon FRIEDL and Theresia SCHMOCZER, on 29 Apr 1849 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Susanna FRIEDL was born on 5 Aug 1827 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 3 and died on 5 Dec 1886 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)
