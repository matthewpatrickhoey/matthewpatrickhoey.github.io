Theresia married Joannes FRINDT, son of Joannes FRINDT and Anna FRIEDL, on 6 Nov 1836 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Joannes FRINDT was born on 11 May 1813 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 2 and died on 7 Mar 1852 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 4.)


Theresia next married Jacobus TISCHLER on 19 May 1858 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Jacobus TISCHLER was born on 25 Apr 1818 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 5.)


Theresia next married Georgius SCHÜRGER, son of Georgius SCHÜRGER and Sophia FRIEDL, on 27 Jun 1867 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Georgius SCHÜRGER was born on 27 Apr 1822 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2.)
