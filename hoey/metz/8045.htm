Andreas HUBLER

  • Born: Abt 1810 1
  • Marriage (1): Catharina GRENCZER on 28 Oct 1832 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1
  • Marriage (2): Julianna MEDER on 18 Jan 1835 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1

Andreas married Catharina GRENCZER, daughter of Josephus GRENCZER and Anna TOPLER, on 28 Oct 1832 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Catharina GRENCZER was born on 30 Oct 1808 in Unter-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary 1 2.)


Andreas next married Julianna MEDER, daughter of Philippus MEDER and Anna KNISNER, on 18 Jan 1835 in Ober-Metzenseifen, Austria-Hungary.1 (Julianna MEDER was born about 1815 1.)
