Campolieto Marriage Records

The following table is an extract of information from the Campolieto Marriage Records available on LDS microfilm. This initial listing covers the years from 1847 through 1864.

I will be adding additional records as I go through the LDS microfilm and as time permits. The surnames I was primarily interested in were Ialenti and Mattarocchia, however other surnames are also included. If you find anything of interest please contact me for more information.

Please note any errors or ommissions are strictly mine in trying to transcribe information from handwritten Italian records. If you find any mistakes (or better yet if you would like to add information), please let me know.
