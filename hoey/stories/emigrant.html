Metzenseifen Emmigrant List

During the late 1870's through the end of the century Unter and Ober Metzenseifen experienced a large wave of emigration to the United States, mainly the towns of Cleveland, OH and Pittsburgh, PA.

Below is a list of 405 known emigrants from both towns (103 from Unter Metzenseifen and 302 from Ober Metzenseifen) with a link to their page on this site for further information. If you have information on someone who should be on the list please let me know.
